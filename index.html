About

This page now contains only your personal and academic profile summary.

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Kent State University. My work focuses on secure and trustworthy AI systems, with particular emphasis on federated learning, privacy-preserving machine learning, fairness-aware modeling, transparency, and explainability.

My goal is to build AI systems that are reliable, interpretable, and secure for sensitive domains such as healthcare, cybersecurity, and distributed decision-making environments.
