Professional Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Kent State University. My academic and research work centers on secure and trustworthy AI systems, with a strong emphasis on federated learning, privacy-preserving machine learning, ethical AI, fairness, transparency, and explainability.

I am particularly interested in designing reliable intelligent systems that can operate responsibly in sensitive and distributed environments such as healthcare, cybersecurity, and edge-enabled systems.
